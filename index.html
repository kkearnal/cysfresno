Parenting Classes

Parenting programs at CYS include our Consider the Children: Co-Parenting Class, which is designed for parents who are separated or divorced. The program examines the needs of children during and after the break-up of a marriage or relationship. Parents learn how to improve the sharing of parental responsibilities for their children.
